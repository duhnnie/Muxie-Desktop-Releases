# Privacy Policy

![](./assets/logo.png)

**Last updated:** March 13, 2026

## Scope of This Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the **Muxie desktop application for macOS**, including all features, functionalities, updates, and future versions of the application.

The policy describes how information is handled when the application is installed and used on a user's device. It applies only to the behavior of the Muxie application itself.

This Privacy Policy does not apply to third-party services that Muxie interacts with, including Last.fm. Such services operate under their own privacy policies and terms, which users should review separately.

## 1. Introduction

Muxie is a desktop application for macOS designed to scrobble music listening activity to Last.fm. This Privacy Policy explains how Muxie handles user information and clarifies what data is (and is not) collected, stored, or transmitted by the application.

Muxie is designed with privacy in mind. The application does not collect, store, analyze, or monetize user data beyond what is strictly necessary to perform its core functionality: submitting listening activity ("scrobbles") to Last.fm.

## 2. Account Authentication

Muxie connects to your Last.fm account using the official authentication process provided by Last.fm.

Muxie **does not have access to your Last.fm username or password** at any time. Authentication is handled through Last.fm’s authorization system, which provides Muxie with a session token allowing the application to submit scrobbles on your behalf.

Muxie does not store or process user credentials.

## 3. Data Transmitted to Last.fm

In order to perform its primary function, Muxie sends listening information to the Last.fm API. This may include:

- Track title
- Artist name
- Album name (if available)
- Album artist (if available)
- Track timestamp
- Playback metadata required for scrobbling

This data is transmitted directly to Last.fm using their official API.

Muxie does not send this data to any other service.

## 4. Data Collection

Muxie **does not collect personal data**, analytics, usage statistics, telemetry, or behavioral tracking information.

The application does not monitor user activity outside of what is necessary to detect and process music playback events for scrobbling purposes.

Muxie does not track users across applications, services, or devices.

## 5. Local Processing and Temporary Storage

Certain data may be processed or temporarily stored locally on your device in order to perform core features, including:

- **Offline scrobble queue:** When the computer is offline, pending scrobbles may be temporarily stored locally until an internet connection is available, at which point they are submitted to Last.fm.
- **Device log parsing:** For supported devices (such as iPod or other players that generate `.scrobbler.log` files), Muxie reads only the specific files required to extract playback information.

These operations occur **entirely on the user’s device**.

Muxie does not transmit or share this locally processed data with any party other than Last.fm for scrobbling purposes.

## 6. File Access (Device Scrobbling)

For device-based scrobbling features (e.g., iPod or portable music players), Muxie scans only the files necessary to obtain playback history.

Muxie does **not scan unrelated files**, personal documents, or other content on your device.

## 7. Data Sharing and Sale

Muxie does **not sell, rent, share, or disclose user data** to third parties.

User listening data is transmitted solely to Last.fm as part of the scrobbling process.

Muxie does not participate in advertising networks, data brokerage, or third-party analytics systems.

## 8. Third-Party Services

Muxie interacts with the following third-party service:

- **Last.fm** — used for submitting scrobbles and synchronizing listening activity.

Use of Last.fm is subject to their own privacy policies and terms of service.

## 9. Data Storage

Muxie does not maintain external servers or databases containing user information.

Any temporary data required for application functionality is stored locally on the user's device.

## 10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect improvements to the application or changes in functionality.

When updates are made, the "Last updated" date at the top of this document will be revised.

## 11. Contact

If you have questions regarding this Privacy Policy or how Muxie handles data, please contact the developer through the official project channels or repository.
